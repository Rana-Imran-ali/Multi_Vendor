FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?
Log in to your account, go to My Orders, and click on any order to see real-time tracking status and estimated delivery date.
Can I return a product?
Yes! All products are covered by our 30-day return policy. Simply go to your order, click "Request Return", and follow the instructions. Refunds are processed within 3–5 business days.
Is my payment information secure?
Absolutely. All payments are processed through Stripe with 256-bit SSL encryption. We never store your card details on our servers.
How do I become a vendor?
Visit our Vendor Center, fill out the application form, and our team will review it within 24 hours. Once approved, you can start listing products immediately.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, and Cash on Delivery in select regions. Apple Pay and Google Pay are also supported.
How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping takes 3–5 business days. Express shipping (1–2 days) is available at checkout for an additional fee. Free standard shipping on orders over $50.
